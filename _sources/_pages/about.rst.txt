PyASGE
=====================
A simple framework for creating 2D games.

This is the official documentation for the AwesomeSauce GameEngine. ASGE is a
games framework developed by James Huxtable and is specifically targeted at
students. It's designed to allow games to be quickly and easily prototyped,
whilst providing the minimum amount of hand-holding. It **specifically** aims to
reduce the complexity of larger game engine and frameworks.
